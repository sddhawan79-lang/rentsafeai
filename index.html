The law is changing

The Renters Rights Act is here.
Are you protected?

Section 21 is gone. The Decent Homes Standard now applies. Awaab's Law creates repair deadlines. Landlords who aren't prepared face heavy fines — and invalid tenancies.

🚫

Section 21 abolished

No-fault evictions are gone. You must now use Section 8 grounds with proper evidence, procedure, and documentation.

🏠

Decent Homes Standard

Private rentals must now meet minimum conditions. Inspections and enforcement are increasing across the UK.

⏱

Awaab's Law — repair deadlines

Mandatory timescales for hazard repairs. Every maintenance issue now carries legal time pressure and liability.
