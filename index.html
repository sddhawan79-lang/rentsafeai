Why I built this

A landlord frustrated by paperwork.
Not a tech company.

I manage a small portfolio of properties and when the Renters Rights Act came in, I spent weeks trying to understand what I actually needed to do differently. Every answer led to more questions, more forms, and more solicitor fees.

The tools that existed were either built for large agencies or hadn't been updated since before Section 21 was abolished. Nothing was built for someone like me — a working landlord trying to stay legal without spending hours on paperwork every month.

So I built RentSafeAI. The AI handles the legal complexity. The audit trail protects you in disputes. And the whole thing takes under 10 minutes to set up. If you're a landlord in 2026, this is the tool I wish had existed.

Founder, RentSafeAI · UK landlord since 2018

Yes — MTD Tax is included on all plans at no extra cost. From 6 April 2026, landlords with gross income over £50,000 must submit quarterly digital records to HMRC.
