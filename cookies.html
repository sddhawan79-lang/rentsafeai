1What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website or use a web application. They are widely used to make websites function correctly, to remember your preferences, and — in some cases — to track user behaviour for analytics or advertising purposes.

This Cookie Notice explains what cookies RentSafe AI uses, why we use them, and what your rights are in relation to them. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions.

5Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that if you delete or block our authentication cookies, you will not be able to log in to the Service and core functionality will be unavailable.

Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

For more information about cookies and how to manage them, visit allaboutcookies.org or the ICO's guidance at ico.org.uk.
